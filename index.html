Privacy Policy Effective: 2026-02-16

Summary: Cows & Bulls Super is designed to collect as little personal information as possible. The app works without creating a traditional account (no email/password). If you choose to use cloud sync or view/submit leaderboards, the app uses Firebase (Google) services to store and retrieve game data linked to an anonymous identifier.

What we collect

  • Display name (the name you enter for leaderboards).
  • Gameplay scores and statistics (e.g., best score, streaks, win time, games played).
  • Anonymous identifier (UID) generated by Firebase Authentication (Anonymous sign-in) to associate your cloud data.
  • Purchase status for the one-time “Super Pro Lifetime” unlock (handled through Apple In-App Purchase and RevenueCat).

What we do not collect

  • No email address, phone number, or real-world identity is required by the app.
  • No advertising ID tracking.
  • No third-party ads SDKs.

Where your data is stored

  • On-device: Core gameplay progress/statistics are stored locally on your device.
  • iCloud (optional): Some data may sync via your private iCloud backup/settings depending on your device configuration.
  • Cloud (only if you use Sync / Hall of Fame): If you choose to sync or use leaderboards (“Hall of Fame”), data is stored in Firebase Firestore under your anonymous UID.

Leaderboards (Hall of Fame)

Leaderboards show only:

  • your display name
  • your score

No email or contact information is shown in leaderboards.

Third-party services

  • Firebase (Google): authentication (anonymous) and Firestore storage for cloud sync/leaderboards.
  • RevenueCat: purchase management for the one-time unlock (no subscriptions).
  • Apple: In-App Purchase processing.

Analytics and tracking

We do not integrate advertising trackers. If analytics are added in a future version, this policy will be updated and disclosures will be made in the store listing as required.

Account & Data Deletion

Important: If you use cloud sync/leaderboards, some data may exist in Firebase linked to your anonymous UID. Deleting the app alone may not remove cloud data.

In-app deletion: A “Delete Cloud Account & Data” option is planned for an upcoming update to allow you to delete cloud-stored data directly from the app.

Until then: Email cowsandbullssuper.app@gmail.com with your request, and we will help you remove cloud data associated with your device/account.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
