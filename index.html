Privacy Policy

Data Protection

Cows & Bulls Super stores gameplay data locally on your device or in your private iCloud storage. We do not store personal data on external servers.

Zero Tracking

This app does not use third-party tracking, advertising SDKs, or analytics systems that collect personal information.

Information Collection

No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected. In-app purchases are securely handled by Apple and RevenueCat using anonymized identifiers.

External Links

The app may provide links to Apple or RevenueCat services. Their privacy policies may differ from ours.

Account & Data Deletion

All game data is stored locally or in your private iCloud account. To delete your data:

  • Delete the app from your device, or
  • Disable iCloud storage for the app in iOS Settings.
