Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-16

Summary

  • We do not collect your email address or phone number.
  • We do not use third-party advertising SDKs.
  • We use Firebase (Firestore + Anonymous Authentication) only to support optional cloud sync and leaderboards.
  • In-app purchases are processed by Apple; purchase infrastructure is handled via RevenueCat.

Data We Collect

The app may process the following types of data depending on which features you use:

1) Local gameplay data (on-device)

  • Gameplay statistics (e.g., games played, wins, streaks, scores, timings)
  • App settings (e.g., difficulty, haptics/sound preferences)

This data is stored locally on your device. If iCloud is enabled for the app, Apple may sync some app data via iCloud according to your device settings.

2) Optional cloud data (Firebase)

If you choose to use cloud features (for example “Sync” or “Hall of Fame/World Rankings”), the app may store:

  • Anonymous user identifier (UID): a random ID created by Firebase Anonymous Authentication
  • Leaderboard entry: your chosen display name and score(s) for the relevant game mode(s)
  • Cloud sync stats: gameplay statistics used to restore progress across devices (if you use Sync)
  • Timestamps: used for syncing and conflict resolution

We do not store your email address in Firebase. Firebase may process device and network information as part of its normal operation.

3) Purchases (Apple + RevenueCat)

  • Purchases are processed by Apple. We do not receive your payment information.
  • RevenueCat helps validate purchases and may process technical identifiers needed for entitlement verification.

Tracking & Analytics

Cows & Bulls Super does not use third-party trackers or advertising SDKs. We do not sell personal data.

How Leaderboards Work

Leaderboards are shown only inside the app when you open Hall of Fame/World Rankings. Your leaderboard entry contains: display name (what you choose) and score.

Data Retention

  • On-device data: remains on your device until you delete the app (or clear app data).
  • iCloud-synced app data: controlled by your iOS/iPadOS iCloud settings.
  • Firebase cloud data: retained until deleted (see the deletion section below).

Account & Data Deletion

Because the app uses anonymous authentication, you may never create a traditional “account” (no email/password required). However, if you used cloud features, an anonymous UID and related cloud data may exist.

A) Delete on-device data

  • Delete the app from your device to remove local data.
  • If iCloud is enabled for the app, you can disable iCloud for the app in iOS Settings to stop syncing.

B) Delete cloud data (Firebase)

To request deletion of your cloud data, email us at cowsandbullssuper.app@gmail.com with the subject line: Delete Cloud Data.

Include (if available):

  • Your in-app display name (the name shown on the leaderboard)
  • Approximate date/time you used Sync or Hall of Fame
  • A screenshot of the Settings screen showing “Connected” (if visible)

We will delete your leaderboard entries and associated cloud stats tied to your anonymous UID.

External Services

  • RevenueCat (purchase entitlement validation)
  • Apple (App Store payments, device/iCloud features)

Their privacy practices may differ from ours. Please review their policies if desired.
