Privacy Policy

1. Overview

Cows & Bulls Super respects your privacy. This app is designed to minimize data collection and operate primarily using local device storage.

2. Information We Collect

The app does not collect personally identifiable information such as name, email address, phone number, or location.

The following limited data may be stored:

  • Anonymous User ID: Generated by Firebase Authentication when you choose to sync or access the leaderboard.
  • Gameplay Statistics: Games played, win percentage, streaks, best scores, and related performance data.
  • Leaderboard Data: Your chosen display name and best score for each game mode (Classic, Challenge, Master, Extreme).
  • Purchase Status: Super Pro lifetime unlock status processed securely via Apple In-App Purchase and RevenueCat.

3. How Data Is Stored

  • Gameplay statistics are stored locally on your device.
  • If you use the Sync feature, selected data is stored securely in Google Firebase Firestore.
  • Leaderboard entries are publicly visible inside the app but are not accessible outside the app.
  • No advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, or third-party analytics tools are used.

4. Account & Data Deletion

You have full control over your data.

  • Delete Cloud Account & Data (Recommended):
    Inside the app, go to Settings → “Delete Cloud Account & Data”.
    This permanently deletes:
    • Your Firebase anonymous account
    • Your stored statistics
    • Your leaderboard entries for all four modes
    This action is irreversible.
  • Reset Local Statistics:
    Use “Reset All Stats” to clear statistics stored on your device only.
  • Remove Local Data:
    Deleting the app from your device removes locally stored data.
If you need assistance with deletion, contact:

cowsandbullssuper.app@gmail.com

5. Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed to children under the age of 13. The game may contain words not suitable for young children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Third-Party Services

The app uses:

  • Apple In-App Purchases (for lifetime unlock)
  • RevenueCat (purchase validation and entitlement management)
  • Google Firebase (anonymous authentication and leaderboard storage)
These services may process limited anonymized identifiers necessary for functionality. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for additional information.

7. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
